===========================
Collection: MiniCosmos.txt

 Minicosmos Levels, created by Aymeric du Peloux. Aymeric has created another level set in the tradition of Microcosmos. He says this set is for beginners because the levels have only 2 or 3 stones. But I still found them interesting, even though I'm an experienced player. Many of the levels come in pairs: a relatively easy level with 2 stones, followed by a more challenging level with the same layout and an extra stone. This is a nice way of providing hints. Original level website: http://www.multimania.com/nabokos/minicosmos.html" sourceURL="http://sneezingtiger.com/sokoban/levels.html
